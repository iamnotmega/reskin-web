## Introduction

At Reskin, we are committed to protecting your privacy.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our app and services.

## Information We Collect and Use

We collect the following information from you to provide and improve our service:

    - **Email Address**: We use your email address to create and secure your account, to reset your password, and to inform you of important updates or moderation actions.

    - **Username**: Your username is collected for your public profile on the Marketplace to identify your uploaded themes.

    - **Password**: Your password is used to authenticate your account and is stored securely in encrypted form.

    - **IP Address**: Your IP address is collected as part of your session information for security purposes and to prevent abuse.


## How We Share Your Information

We share your data with Appwrite, a third-party service that provides the backend infrastructure for Reskin's user accounts, authentication, and Marketplace.
We share this data to operate our service and provide you with a seamless experience.
